WELCOME TO MY HOME PAGE

Hello! I am Zeliha I’ve been living in Manchester for two years. I graduated from a Turkish University with a bachelor’s degree. During my career I worked as a CRM specialist and as a teacher for several years. My experience in the CRM field has provided me with valuable insights into how technology can be used to improve customer engagement and streamline business processes. I have continued to explore the field of computer science and have tried to learn some software languages by myself and really enjoyed it a lot. Here in the UK I decided to build a career in software development.

What I find most exciting about being a developer is the constantly evolving nature of the field. As we all know global software industry is huge, and it is growing rapidly. There are lots of benefits of becoming a software developer such as quick career progression, higher job satisfaction rates, high demand and excellent salary, the ability to have a flexible lifestyle, and the opportunity to change the world. And now it's my time! WOOW! I have passion for it and I believe in myself.
